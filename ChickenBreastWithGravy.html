Chicken Breasts with Chipotle Green Onion Gravy

Total time: 20 minutes

Ingredients

4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
salt and fresh ground pepper to taste
2 tablespoons olive oil
1/4 cup butter
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1-1/2 cups chicken broth
1/4 cup minced green onions
1 teaspoon chipotle chile powder, or more to taste

Directions

1. Place chicken breast halves between two sheets of heavy plastic (resealable freezer bags work well) on a solid, level surface.

2. Firmly pound chicken with the smooth side of a meat mallet to a thickness of 1/2-inch.

3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

4. Heat olive oil in a skillet on high heat until it begins to shimmer, about 1 minute.

5. Reduce heat to medium; cook chicken breasts until browned and no longer pink inside, about 5 minutes per side.

6. Transfer cooked chicken to a warm plate and loosely cover with aluminum foil.

7. Melt butter in the skillet; stir in flour and cook for about 2 minutes.

8. Stir in chicken broth, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom of the pan; cook and stir until gravy begins to simmer and thicken, 1 to 2 minutes.

9. Stir in green onions and chipotle chile powder.

10. Return chicken breasts to the skillet and cook until heated through, 1 to 2 minutes.
